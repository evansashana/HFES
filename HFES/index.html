HFES Executive Board 2016-2017

Get to know your E-Board!

Janetta Brown, President

Janetta Brown is a Ph.D. student in the Industrial and Systems Engineering department at North Carolina A&T State University. A few of her hobbies include traveling, reading, and sport.

Harshitha Medae, Vice President

Harshitha Meda is a second year Master’s degree student in the Industrial and Systems Engineering department at North Carolina A&T. Hailing from Hyderabad, India, she earned her undergraduate degree in electrical engineering. When she is not working hard in the HFA Laboratory, she enjoys reading books, dancing, cooking, and listening to music in her spare time.

Trevor K. Downey, Communications Chair

Trevor is a Senior Industrial and Systems Engineering (ISE) major from Durham, NC. He is presently working under CASIS on Behavioral Scripts Research at North Carolina A&T State University. Besides his current involvement with HFES, Trevor is also in search of his next internship or co-op within the ISE field.

ShaTara Tarte, Treasurer

ShaTara is currently a graduate student at NCAT pursuing her Masters degree in Industrial Engineering. Serving as a Teaching Assistant for General Engineering 100, ShaTara has the opportunity to inspire and educate others. This quest for consistent growth is evidenced by her efforts to continue learning outside of what is required.
